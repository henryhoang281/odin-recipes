Oven roasted lemon-thyme chicken

Description:

Oven roasted lemon-thyme chicken for family day.

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 2 whole lemon
  • 1 bunch of thyme
  • 1 liter chicken stock
  • Salt and pepper

Steps:

  1. Season chicken with salt and pepper and chopped thyme
  2. Roast in the oven 350C for 30 minutes
  3. Make the sauce from reducing chicken stock and lemon juice and thyme
  4. Once the sauce reduced season to taste
  5. Serve the chicken with the sauce

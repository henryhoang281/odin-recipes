Chicken Parmesan

Description:

Classic Italian cuisine chicken parmesan.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breast
  • 20g AP flour
  • 20g bread crumb
  • 2 clove garlic
  • 1 whole egg
  • Tomato sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps:

  1. Sweat garlic in olive oil then add tomato sauce, simmer for 15 minutes
  2. While waiting for tomato sauce to simmer, prepare chicken
  3. Butterfly chicken breast then season with salt and pepper
  4. Coat the chicken with flour, then egg wash, then bread crumb
  5. Fry 2 sides of the chicken, 5 minutes each until golden brown
  6. When done, take the chicken breast out and sprinkle parm cheese all over the chicken
  7. Pour tomato sauce on top, then sprinkle more parm cheese and serve
